Keep items in singular, lowercase, and without accents to make autocomplete easy to use.

category
 - category_id
 - name
 Example: 'food', 'cleaning', 'houseware'

product
 - product_id
 - category_id (fk)
 - name
 Example: 'onion'
 
store
 - store_id
 - name
 Example: 'mercadinho'

purchase
 - purchase_id
 - product_id (fk)
 - store_id (fk)
 - event_date
 - qty
 - unit ('kg', 'l', 'unit')
 - price
 - notes
 Example: onion,mercadinho,2020-11-06,0.821,kg,3.23,'sale'
